Connect!

Ninastech is a creative project dedicated to supporting girls studying programming. Additionally, we offer website creation services for companies.

What Can We Do for Companies?

If you're interested in creating your business website or app, we provide freelance consultancy services for companies.

  • Software Development Services

    We can create your business website or app using agile methodologies. Following that, we develop custom software solutions and provide regular updates to the client throughout the development process.

  • UX & UI Design

    Ninastech can help you with every aspect of UX design, from user research and user interface (UI) design to usability testing. We can create a visually appealing design that are intuitive, accessible, and engaging.

  • Mobile App development

    Our mobile app development services include iOS and Android app development. Ninastech can help you bring your mobile app idea to life, from concept and design to development and launch.

What Can We Offer Developers?

If you are a student or a programmer searching for a job, we can assist with connections, information, and study group projects.

  • Ninastech Consulting

    Elevate your career and become an expert software engineering consultant. You have the opportunity to collaborate with Ninastech as a freelancer, leveraging our community to work on projects and acquire valuable experience.(You don't need to be a volunteer to be a part of Nina's Consulting.)

  • Voluntary Experience

    Working as a voluntary software engineer at Ninastech offers an exceptional opportunity to create a positive impact in the community. By applying your skills and expertise to meaningful projects, you can actively gain the experience that all engineers need.We are open to new ideas for improving our project.

  • Your business idea

    Ninastech wants that you achieve your independence being as a programmer, or working for your own business. If you have an idea or project, we will help you. Give week tasks to give your fisrt steps building websites until get the courage you need for a opportunity.

Why Work With Us?

We work as a team of mentor volunteers that can help you until you are confident to get the job!

  • Volunteering will provide you with a space to learn, make mistakes, improve and grow. Is a very rich opportunity to learn new skills and also teach others around you. Companies look for years of experience, the longer you work in the field, the more chances you get to find an interview opportunity.

  • Volunteering gives you the opportunity to learn more about the tasks of that position. Find developers to collaborate with and make new friends from other countries.

  • It's an incredible opportunity to work with people of different cultures and make connections. With these experiences, as you progress in your career, you will be able to tell the stories of these learnings in interviews and also use these skills in industry positions in USA.
