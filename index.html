Connect!

Ninastech is a volunteer community that provides support and assistance in creating connections for careers in the tech industry.

What we do?

We provide assistance with networking and information.

  • Instagram Ninastech

    Ninastech utilizes Instagram as a powerful tool for visual learning of programming. With clear and concise pictures, we provide an easy-to-understand explanation of programming concepts, making the programming glossary more accessible and simplifying the learning process.

  • Youtube Ninastech

    Ninastech dedicated to inspiring individuals to learn programming through engaging videos. We provide step-by-step tutorials that break down complex concepts into simple, easy-to-understand explanations. Additionally, we offer translations of our knowledge into Portuguese.

  • Linkedin Ninastech

    Connect with us and find opportunities as potential employers, recruiters, and influencers. Take advantage to social media on Ninastech and build a robust professional network. This network has the potential to open doors to thrilling career prospects in the programming field.

  • Discord Ninastech

    Join a vibrant Ninastech community of programming learners on Discord. Connect with fellow enthusiasts, exchange ideas, and engage in discussions around programming topics. Share your insights, ask questions, and collaborate on coding challenges.Start your programming journey today!

  • Web development

    Ninastech specialize in creating fast, user-friendly websites that are optimized for search engines.

  • UX Design

    We create stunning, responsive websites that showcase your brand and engage your audience.

  • Mobile Solutions

    Ninastech develops mobile solutions and provides mobile application development services.

  • Ninastech Consulting

    If you are a student, accelerate your career and become an expert software engineering consultant

Why Work With Us?

We work as a team of mentor volunteers that can help you until you are confident to get the job!

  • Volunteering will provide you with a space to learn, make mistakes, improve and grow. Is a very rich opportunity to learn new skills and also teach others around you. Companies look for years of experience, the longer you work in the field, the more chances you get to find an interview opportunity.

  • Volunteering gives you the opportunity to learn more about the tasks of that position. Find developers to collaborate with and make new friends from other countries.

  • It's an incredible opportunity to work with people of different cultures and make connections. With these experiences, as you progress in your career, you will be able to tell the stories of these learnings in interviews and also use these skills in industry positions in USA.
