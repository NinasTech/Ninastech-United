What We Do?

Ninastech is a non-profit startup dedicated to supporting girls studying programming. Additionally, we offer website creation services for companies.

  • Software Development

    We begin by understanding our client's business objectives and identifying their software development needs. Using agile methodologies, we then develop custom software solutions and provide regular updates to the client throughout the development process.

  • Project Management

    Our expert project management and delivery services ensure that your projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. We can help you manage every aspect of your project, from planning and execution to monitoring and control.

  • Create Your Business Website

    Ninastech wants that you achieve your independence being as a programmer, or working for your own business. If you have an idea or project, we will help you. Give week tasks to give your fisrt steps building websites until get the courage you need for a opportunity.

  • Ninastech Consulting

    Advance your career and become an expert software engineer consultant with our creative and practical course. Learn from industry professionals and gain hands-on experience with the latest tools and techniques in software engineering consulting. Join us!

Why Work With Us?

We work as a team of mentor volunteers that can help you until you are confident to get the job!

  • Volunteering will provide you with a space to learn, make mistakes, improve and grow. Is a very rich opportunity to learn new skills and also teach others around you. Companies look for years of experience, the longer you work in the field, the more chances you get to find an interview opportunity.

  • Volunteering gives you the opportunity to learn more about the tasks of that position. Find developers to collaborate with and make new friends from other countries.

  • It's an incredible opportunity to work with people of different cultures and make connections. With these experiences, as you progress in your career, you will be able to tell the stories of these learnings in interviews and also use these skills in industry positions in USA.
